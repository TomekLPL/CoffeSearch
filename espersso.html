Espresso

About this exquisite coffee:

Originating from Italy, espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.

Its name, meaning "pressed out" or "on demand," reflects the quick preparation method. Espresso is known for its rich and intense flavor, achieved through a short brewing time, typically around 25-30 seconds.

The hallmark golden crema on top adds a layer of complexity, and its serving size of about 30 ml makes it a potent and beloved shot in the world of coffee.

Whether enjoyed on its own or as a base for various coffee creations, espresso stands as a testament to the art and science of coffee brewing.
