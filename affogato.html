Affogato

About this exquisite coffee:

Affogato is a delightful Italian coffee-based dessert or beverage. It consists of a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato "drowned" or "affogato" in a shot of hot espresso. The hot espresso melts the cold ice cream, creating a contrasting combination of temperatures and textures.

The result is a delicious blend of rich, bold coffee flavors with the sweetness and creaminess of the ice cream. Affogato is known for its simplicity and is often enjoyed as a refreshing and indulgent treat, especially during warmer weather.
