As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, there wasn't a widely recognized or standardized preparation known as "nitro French press coffee." However, the coffee industry is dynamic and continually evolving, and new methods and trends emerge.

A traditional French press involves steeping coarsely ground coffee in hot water and then separating the grounds by pressing a plunger through a mesh filter. If there have been developments since my last update, a "nitro French press" could potentially refer to a variation where nitrogen is infused into coffee brewed using a French press.
