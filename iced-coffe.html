About this exquisite coffee:

Iced coffee is a cold coffee beverage that is served over ice. It is made by brewing hot coffee and then cooling it down, either by pouring it over ice or refrigerating it. Iced coffee can be sweetened and flavored according to personal preferences, and it is often served with milk or cream.

There are various methods for making iced coffee, including brewing it hot and then chilling it or using cold brew techniques where coffee grounds are steeped in cold water for an extended period. Iced coffee is a popular and refreshing option, especially in warmer weather, offering a chilled and customizable alternative to traditional hot coffee.
